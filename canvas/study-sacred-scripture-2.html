God Reveals Himself to Us

  1. - Psalm 19:1 - The heavens declare God's glory, the skies proclaim His handiwork.
  2. - Romans 1:20 - God's divine nature is evident from creation. 
  3. - Psalm 147:19 - God declares His word and statutes to Israel.
  4. - Hebrews 1:1-2 - God spoke through OT prophets and supremely through Christ.
  5. - John 1:14 - Christ is the incarnate Word, revealing God's glory. 
  6. - John 14:9 - Whoever has seen Jesus has seen the Father.
  7. - 2 Timothy 3:16 - Scripture is God-breathed, useful for teaching and training.
  8. - 1 Corinthians 2:10 - The Spirit reveals God's truth to believers.
  9. - Romans 10:17 - Hearing the gospel message reveals God.
  10. - Exodus 3:13-14 - God revealed His name Yahweh to Moses at the burning bush.
  11. - Psalm 103:7 - God made known His ways to Moses.
  12. - Matthew 11:27 - Jesus reveals the Father to those to whom He wills.
  13. - Ephesians 1:17 - Paul prays for the Spirit of wisdom and revelation about God. 
  14. - Hebrews 3:7-11 - The Holy Spirit spoke through Scripture's authors.
  15. - Psalm 78:5-7 - Fathers teach the next generation God's laws.
  16. - Acts 14:17 - God's goodness is evident in providing rain, crops, and joyful hearts. 
  17. - Romans 2:15 - The moral law of God is written on people's hearts.
  18. - Psalm 8:1 - God reveals His majesty through the heavens and through children's praise.
  19. - Romans 16:25-27 - The prophetic Scriptures reveal the mystery of God's plan.
  20. - Matthew 16:17 - God the Father revealed Jesus' identity to Peter.
