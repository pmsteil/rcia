This video explores the ancient and biblical roots of Catholicism, contrasting the Evangelical and Catholic perspectives.

The speaker, Keith Little, a former evangelical seeking to understand Catholicism, is joined by Dr. John Bergsma, a former Protestant pastor turned Catholic priest.

Dr. Bergsma explains that Catholicism, unlike Evangelicalism, views the Old Covenant as fulfilled, not abolished, in the New Covenant. This means elements like priesthood, sacrifice, and other Old Testament rituals continue in the Catholic Church.

He delves into the early church fathers who shaped Catholicism, highlighting their belief in the Eucharist's real presence, the authority of bishops, and the sacraments' significance.

Finally, Dr. Bergsma explores the Jewish roots of Catholicism, revealing how many Catholic practices and beliefs, like incense use, vestments, and specific feast celebrations, stem from Judaism.

This video offers valuable insights for anyone interested in Catholicism and serves as a reminder of its rich and lengthy history.
