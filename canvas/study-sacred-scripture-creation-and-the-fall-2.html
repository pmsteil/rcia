Creation and the Fall

1. Genesis 1:1-31 - God creates the heavens, the earth, and all living creatures in 6 days.

2. Genesis 2:4-25 - God creates Adam and Eve and places them in the Garden of Eden.

3. Genesis 3:1-24 - The serpent tempts Eve, she and Adam eat the forbidden fruit, and are banished from the garden.

4. Job 38-42 - God speaks to Job about his wisdom in creation and power over the universe. 

5. Psalm 8:3-9 - David praises God's majesty in creating the heavens, moon, stars, and mankind. 

6. Psalm 19:1-6 - The heavens declare the glory of God, displaying his handiwork.

7. Psalm 104 - God created all things wisely and sustains all creation. 

8. Proverbs 8:22-31 - Wisdom was present at creation and rejoiced with God.

9. Isaiah 40:25-28 - The incomparable Creator God gives strength to the weak.

10. Isaiah 42:5 - God created the heavens and spread out the earth by his power. 

11. Isaiah 45:18 - God created the heavens and earth to be inhabited.

12. Mark 10:6-9 - Jesus refers to God making mankind male and female from the beginning of creation.

13. Luke 11:50-51 - Jesus refers to righteous blood shed since the foundation of the world, implying a Biblical timeline back to creation. 

14. John 1:1-5 - Jesus Christ, the Word, was with God at creation and all things were made through him. 

15. John 5:17 - Jesus declares that His Father is working until now, continuing to sustain creation.

16. John 8:44 - Jesus calls the devil a murderer from the beginning and father of lies, referring to Genesis and the fall.

17. John 17:24 - Jesus prays that He may be with the Father, as He was at the foundation of the world. 

18. Acts 17:24-28 - God created everything and determines the times and places we live.

19. Romans 1:18-25 - God's invisible attributes are clearly seen in creation, yet mankind rejects him. 

20. Romans 5:12-14 - Sin entered the world through Adam and brought death. 

21. Romans 8:18-25 - Creation is subjected to futility because of sin but will be restored.

22. 1 Corinthians 15:20-28, 35-49 - Through Adam came death, through Christ comes resurrection.

23. 2 Corinthians 4:6 - The glory of God is seen in the face of Jesus Christ. 

24. Ephesians 3:9 - God created all things through Jesus Christ. 

25. Colossians 1:15-20 - Christ is the image of God and by him all things were created.

26. Hebrews 1:1-4 - God spoke through his Son through whom he made the universe. 

27. Hebrews 11:3 - By faith we understand the universe was created by God's word.

28. Revelation 4:11 - All creation exists for God's pleasure and will.
