The Most Holy Trinity

1. Genesis 1:26-27 - God speaks in plural form, "Let us make man in our image."

2. Psalm 110:1-4 - David speaks prophetically of the Lord (God) and my Lord (Jesus), foretelling Jesus. 

3. Isaiah 48:12-16 - The Son and Spirit are spoken of alongside the Lord God.

4. Matthew 3:13-17 - Jesus is baptized, the Spirit descends, the Father speaks.

5. Matthew 28:16-20 - Jesus instructs making disciples and baptizing in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

6. John 1:1-18 - The Word (Jesus) was with God and was God.

7. John 10:22-33 - Jesus said "I and the Father are one."

8. John 14:1-11 - Jesus said "Whoever has seen me has seen the Father." 

9. John 14:16-17 - Jesus speaks of asking the Father to send the Holy Spirit.

10. John 15:26-27 - Jesus will send the Holy Spirit, who proceeds from the Father.

11. John 20:24-31 - Thomas calls Jesus "My Lord and my God." 

12. Acts 5:1-11 - Lying to the Holy Spirit means lying to God.

13. Romans 8:1-17 - The Spirit of God and Christ dwell in believers.

14. 1 Corinthians 12:1-11 - Gifts given by Spirit, Lord (Jesus), and God.

15. 2 Corinthians 3:12-18 - The Lord (Jesus) is the Spirit.

16. 2 Corinthians 13:11-14 - Paul invokes Jesus, God, and the Spirit.

17. Ephesians 4:1-6 - One Spirit, one Lord (Jesus), one God and Father. 

18. Colossians 1:15-20 - In Jesus the fullness of deity dwells.

19. 1 Peter 1:2 - Peter speaks of God, the Spirit, and Jesus' blood.

20. 1 Peter 1:10-12 - The Spirit of Christ in the prophets.
